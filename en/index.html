Process

A calm, considered way to buy.

Most buyers find a place in 6–10 weeks with us. Some faster, some slower — the brief dictates the pace, not pressure.

  1. 1

    Strategy call

    A free 20-minute conversation to understand your goals, finance position and non-negotiables.

  2. 2

    Brief & engage

    We lock in a written brief — suburbs, budget, property type, timeline — and formally engage with a fixed fee.

  3. 3

    Search & shortlist

    We tap agent networks for off-market stock and review every listing across your target areas. You see only the best matches.

  4. 4

    Inspect & assess

    We inspect in person with a builder's eye, pull comparable sales, and send you a clear recommendation.

  5. 5

    Negotiate or bid

    We handle the agent, the offer strategy, the contract conditions and — when needed — the auction paddle.

  6. 6

    Settle with confidence

    We coordinate inspections, finance, solicitor and pre-settlement walk-through so nothing falls through the cracks.

Common questions

The things most buyers ask first.

How much does a buyers' agent cost?

Our engagements are fixed-fee, agreed upfront and in writing — no percentage-of-purchase surprises. Fees vary with scope (full search vs. negotiate-only) and property value. Most clients recoup the fee in negotiation savings alone.

Do you take kickbacks from selling agents or developers?

Never. We work exclusively for buyers and don't accept commissions, rebates or referral fees from any third party. It's the core of what makes us independent.

How long does the search take?

Most of our clients are under contract within 6–10 weeks of engagement. Clear briefs in hot sub-markets can be faster; bespoke criteria or softer markets can take longer. We never rush a brief to close a job.

Can you help if I've already found a property?

Yes. Our Evaluate & Negotiate service is designed for exactly that — an independent assessment, risk check and full negotiation without the search component.

Do you work with interstate and overseas buyers?

A large share of our clients are interstate or offshore. We handle inspections, video walk-throughs, contracts, building & pest, and the settlement walk-through on your behalf.
